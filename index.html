About This Website

Welcome to my Neocities page! This site is designed to mimic the nostalgic feeling of installing software in the late 90s and early 2000s.

I'm a web developer with a passion for retro computing and creative interfaces. This site serves as both a portfolio and a playground for experimenting with design concepts that celebrate the golden era of personal computing.

The entire site is built using HTML, CSS, and vanilla JavaScript - no frameworks or libraries were harmed in the making of this website.
